Profit Hunters Coin [PHC]

Profit Hunters Coin (PHC) is a free open source decentralized project derived from Bitcoin, with the goal of providing a long-term energy-efficient scrypt-based crypto-currency. Built on the foundations of Bitcoin, Litecoin, PeerCoin, NovaCoin, CraveProject, Dash, XUVcoin, BATA and Crypostle.
