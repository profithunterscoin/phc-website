Buying and selling PHC

How much are these coins worth?

Network hash-rates determine a large portion of the coin price, as well as difficulty. See Here to monitor the live-network. A general rule is to calculate approx how much electricity is used to generate a coin and increase that by a small profit margin. A coin with long-term use qualities such as PHC, can also be considered a good store of value. Speculation can also be reflected by investors when trading based upon media, or technical improvements of the code base itself.
